About Us

Melomaniac provides a platform to share views on music through words. Listening to Music is an inexplainable feeling each and everyone have different thoughts on a same song,tune or note.

In melomaniac, find and meet people around the world with no barriers. Here one can express any thoughts, opinions, suggestions on the music of your preference. Turn your discussions into a whole new world. Music can be healing to someone ...enjoyment to another..a companion or comfort to some other. So for all music lovers melomaniac is the platform for you.
